# JSON Thing

**The best JSON explorer with support for nested data and complex filtering and sorting.**

### → Try it now at [jsonthing.com](https://jsonthing.com)

Most JSON viewers just pretty‑print a tree and leave you to scroll. JSON Thing treats your data like
what it really is — a set of related records — and turns arbitrary JSON into linked, drill‑down
tables you can actually explore.

Paste anything and it works instantly. No build step, no sign‑up, no upload — everything runs in
your browser and your data never leaves it.

## Why you'll like it

- **Nested data, finally readable.** Every nested array becomes its own table, wired to its parents.
  Go from parent to child and back, with breadcrumbs the whole way.
- **Explore like a database.** Global full‑text search, Notion/Linear‑style advanced filters,
  multi‑column sorting, and faceted filters — at every level of the tree.
- **See the whole story.** Click any entry to open a detail view with its complete ancestry and
  every field laid out.
- **Make it yours.** Any color from the full palette, light or dark, and layout settings that stick.
- **Instant and private.** Live parsing as you type, one self‑contained page, zero telemetry.

Stop scrolling through walls of brackets.
